Background Music With Green Screen

What Is It?

Background Music With Green Screen allows you to embed a URL in your videos which will automatically play high quality music from this youtube video. This also changes the colour of the page containing the music to be the green screen colour.

Which Browsers Does It Support?

The browsers which Background Music Green Screen supports are as follows

Browser Publisher Version Supported
Internet Explorer Microsoft Corporation 9
Edge Microsoft Corporation 3
Firefox Mozilla Corporation 23
Chrome Google LLC 34
Safari Apple Inc 6
